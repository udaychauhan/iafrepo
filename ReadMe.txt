This is an Angular App which uses ice and fire api, to get and show information about books, characters and houses. 

Here is the general desired working of app :

1. The home page consists of all books, 10 characters ( which can be navigated by clicking next/prev button ), 10 houses ( can be navigated same as books ).

2. Whenever user desires to read ingo about the book or character or house he can click button on panel and can view further details about the desired object.
